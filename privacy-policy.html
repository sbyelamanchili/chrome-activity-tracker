Privacy Policy for Activity Time Tracker & Productivity Analytics

Last Updated: December 12, 2024

1. Overview

Activity Time Tracker & Productivity Analytics is a Chrome extension designed to help you monitor and improve your browsing habits. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data with utmost respect for your privacy.

2. Required Permissions

Our extension requires the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage: To store your activity data locally on your device
  • tabs: To count open tabs and track active tab information
  • activeTab: To monitor current tab activity

3. Information We Collect

The extension collects:

  • Number of open tabs
  • Active tab information (domain names only, not full URLs)
  • Time spent on different websites
  • Website categorization data

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. Specifically:

  • Activity data is stored in your browser's local storage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Data is automatically reset at midnight
  • You can manually clear your data at any time

5. Website Categorization

We categorize websites into:

  • Social Media (e.g., Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn)
  • Productivity (e.g., GitHub, Google Docs, Slack)
  • Entertainment (e.g., YouTube, Netflix)
  • News (e.g., Google News, Reuters)

This categorization is done locally and solely for the purpose of providing you with meaningful analytics.

6. User Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • View your activity statistics and tab count
  • Clear all stored data
  • Customize notification preferences
  • Export your activity data

7. Data Security

We implement several security measures:

  • All data remains on your local device
  • No personal information is collected or transmitted
  • Automatic data cleanup at midnight
  • No third-party access to your data

8. Offline Functionality

The extension is fully functional offline, as confirmed by the manifest's offline_enabled setting. No internet connection is required for the extension to track and analyze your browsing habits.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as we enhance the extension with new features. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. Contact Information

For questions about this privacy policy or the extension's privacy practices, please visit our GitHub repository or contact us through the Chrome Web Store.

11. Your Consent

By installing and using Activity Time Tracker & Productivity Analytics, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this privacy policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing your data through the extension's interface.
