Privacy Policy for Chrome Activity Tracker

Last Updated: December 11, 2024

1. Information We Collect

Our extension collects the following information:

  • Website domains visited (not full URLs)
  • Time spent on different websites
  • Number of active tabs
  • Website categories (including social, productivity, entertainment, and news)
  • Daily usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Track and display your daily browsing patterns
  • Generate usage statistics and activity reports
  • Calculate daily averages and most visited sites
  • Help you manage your browsing habits

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API. We do not:

  • Send your data to external servers
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Store personal information beyond browsing statistics

4. User Controls and Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Enable or disable tracking at any time
  • Clear all activity data while keeping settings
  • Export your data
  • Set daily goals and preferences
  • Choose notification preferences

5. Data Retention

The extension includes an automatic data reset feature that runs daily at midnight. You can also manually clear your data at any time through the extension's interface.

6. Website Categorization

We categorize websites into the following groups:

  • Social Media (e.g., Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn)
  • Productivity (e.g., GitHub, Google Docs, Slack)
  • Entertainment (e.g., YouTube, Netflix, Spotify)
  • News (e.g., Google News, Reuters, BBC)

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as we add new features or modify existing ones. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the extension's data practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support channel.

9. Your Consent

By installing and using this extension, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this privacy policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by uninstalling the extension or disabling tracking in the extension settings.
